## 📌 Overview
A data-driven exploration of South Korea’s global influence.

---

## 💡 Key Concept
This project treats Korea not as a single dimension, but as a **system**.

It integrates:

- 📜 History → structural foundation  
- 📊 Economy & Technology → hard power  
- 🎤 Culture & Sports → soft power  
- 🧠 Society → internal dynamics  
- ✈️ Tourism → real-world experience  

👉 The goal is to move from stereotypes to **system-level understanding**.
