# Korea Compass 3-Minute Video Demo Script

## 0:00-0:20 Problem

International students and job seekers researching Korea have to piece together universities, visas, city choice, costs, jobs, language needs, and living logistics from disconnected sources.

## 0:20-0:50 Product Overview

Korea Compass brings those decisions into one bilingual Study / Work / Live planning app. Show the homepage, Explore Korea, Study, Work, Live, Korean Learning, and AI Korea Life Plan flow.

## 0:50-1:30 Profile + City Recommendation

Open Profile Center and show how a reusable profile captures study, career, and living preferences. Then open City Recommendation and show the ranked cities, best city card, score breakdown, and recommendation reason.

## 1:30-2:00 Korean Learning + Living

Open Korean Learning and show a real-life scenario such as interview, classroom, hospital, or subway. Then show the Living MBTI City Match to demonstrate lifestyle-based city fit.

## 2:00-2:30 AI Korea Life Plan

Generate or display an AI Korea Life Plan. Highlight overall recommendation, best city, study path, career path, living plan, budget analysis, risks, visa pathway, language learning plan, and action plans.

## 2:30-3:00 Technical Architecture + Closing

Show Knowledge Base Status and explain the architecture: Streamlit frontend, FastAPI backend, SQLite persistence, JSON Knowledge Base, Source Registry, Plotly visualizations, local AI fallback, and 200+ automated tests.

Close by saying Korea Compass is designed as a portfolio-ready full-stack AI planning product with transparent data provenance and deployment-ready demo modes.
